## Frequently Asked Questions

### What flavors of Mochi Ice Cream do you offer?
Our mochi flavors frequently rotate, but we typically offer popular choices like Green Tea (Matcha), Strawberry, Mango, and Vanilla. Ask your server for today's selection!

### What is Fried Ice Cream?
It is a large scoop of ice cream that is frozen solid, coated in a sweet, crunchy batter (like pound cake or panko), and deep-fried very quickly. It is hot and crispy on the outside and frozen on the inside!

### Do the fried desserts come with any toppings?
Yes, our Fried Bananas and Fried Ice Cream are typically drizzled with chocolate or caramel syrup and finished with whipped cream.

### Are these desserts available for takeout?
Due to melting, we highly recommend enjoying our Fried Ice Cream and regular Ice Cream while dining in. However, Mochi and Fried Bananas can travel short distances!
