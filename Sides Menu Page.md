## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between Steamed Rice and Sushi Rice?
Steamed rice is plain, hot white rice. Sushi rice is a specific short-grain rice that is cooked and then folded with a seasoned vinegar mixture, giving it a slightly sweet and tangy flavor.

### Is the Miso Soup vegetarian?
Traditional Japanese miso soup uses a dashi (fish stock) base. Please ask your server if you have strict dietary requirements!

### Can I substitute Brown Rice for Steamed Rice with my entrée?
Yes! For a small upcharge, you can substitute healthy brown rice or seasoned sushi rice with any of our hot kitchen entrées.

### Are these sides available for takeout?
Absolutely. All of our sides, from hot soups to fresh salads, are packaged securely for easy takeout in San Antonio.
