## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between Masago, Tobiko, and Ikura?
Masago (smelt roe) is the smallest and slightly sweet. Tobiko (flying fish roe) is larger and crunchier. Ikura (salmon roe) is the largest, with a soft texture that bursts with a rich, savory flavor.

### How is the roe served?
You can order our roe as "Sushi" (served over a small bed of seasoned rice, wrapped in a thin strip of nori) or as "Sashimi" (served on its own).

### Can I get roe added to my sushi rolls?
Yes! Many of our signature rolls already include roe, but you can always ask your server to add Masago or Tobiko to the top of any classic roll for an extra crunch.

### Are these items gluten-free?
Roe itself is naturally gluten-free, but marinades sometimes contain soy sauce. Please let us know if you have a gluten allergy so we can prepare your order safely!
