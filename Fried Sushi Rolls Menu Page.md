## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are the ingredients inside the fried rolls raw or cooked?
Because the entire roll is flash-fried in tempura batter or panko, the ingredients inside are generally cooked or significantly warmed through the process.

### Can I get a regular roll deep-fried?
Yes! For a small upcharge, our chefs can tempura-fry almost any classic roll on the menu, like a California or Philadelphia Roll. Just ask your server!

### Are fried rolls good for takeout?
We package our fried rolls carefully to ensure they retain as much of their crunch as possible for the ride home in San Antonio.

### What pairs well with fried rolls?
Since fried rolls are rich and savory, they pair wonderfully with a light side like our Cucumber Salad, Edamame, or a cold Japanese beer.
