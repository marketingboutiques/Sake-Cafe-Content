## Frequently Asked Questions

### What kind of dressing comes with the Garden Salad?
Our Side Salad and House Garden Salad are served with our signature, house-made Japanese ginger dressing.

### Are your salads gluten-free?
Many of our salads, like the Side Salad and Cucumber Salad, are gluten-free or can be made gluten-free. Please let your server know about any dietary restrictions!

### Is the Spicy Tuna Salad served with raw fish?
Yes, the Spicy Tuna Salad features fresh, sushi-grade raw tuna tossed in our house spicy sauce over greens.

Absolutely. All of our salads are packaged carefully to stay crisp and fresh for takeout and delivery in San Antonio.
